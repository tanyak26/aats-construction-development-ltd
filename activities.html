Business activity areas

Real estate support with construction built in.

The company nature covers the operation of owned or leased real estate, property readiness, domestic construction, building completion and specialised construction activity.

Property activity

From real estate intention to ready-to-use spaces.

This page explains the property and construction work in plain language so clients can quickly understand whether their asset, letting or improvement project fits the AATS service profile.

Owned and leased real estate operation.

AATS has a real estate operation dimension. This supports owned or leased property assets, lettable spaces, tenant readiness, occupancy planning, sale preparation and practical decisions that help a property function after construction work ends.

  • Letting-ready spaces
  • Owned property support
  • Leased property use
  • Asset-readiness planning

Domestic building and residential improvement.

AATS can present itself around residential construction work, including domestic property builds, house improvement programmes, layout changes, structural preparation and coordinated works that support comfortable living spaces.

  • Domestic building projects
  • Home alterations
  • Residential refurbishment
  • Build-stage coordination

Building completion and finishing.

Completion and finishing work is where a project becomes usable. This includes the detail stage of a property: finished surfaces, final adjustments, internal completion, practical snagging and preparation before occupation, sale or letting.

  • Internal finishing
  • Final presentation
  • Snagging support
  • Handover preparation

Specialist construction activities.

Some projects need focused construction support that does not fit neatly into a single trade description. AATS can describe specialist activity around access constraints, preparation works, structural support, sequencing, repairs and carefully managed site conditions.

  • Access-led planning
  • Specialist preparation
  • Structural support tasks
  • Coordinated site works
Joined-up purpose

The same asset may need property and construction thinking.

A property may be held, leased, let, sold, occupied or improved over time. AATS now presents real estate readiness first, supported by domestic building, finishing and specialist construction where the asset needs physical work.
